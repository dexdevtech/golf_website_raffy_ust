ABOUT US

San Pasucal Golfers (SPG) was started in 2009 where in members
were all residents of San pascual. It officially became San Pascual
Golfers Association (SPGA) when it extends its membership
outside San Pascual in 2010.

The tournaments and other events were conducted in San
Pascual Golf ourse which was considered as their home course

Location: The San Pascual Golf club is a distinguished golf course
in Batangas. Its operation in 2012. Its short 9-hole course
features flat fairways that are perfect for beginning golfers or
those who choose to walk the course. Aligning the fairways of the
course are three unique trees of the Acacia, Pine, and narra
families. In addition, you will find that the course is surrounded by
narrow ravines, water hazards, and deep ditches that make the
course extra challenging to golfers.
