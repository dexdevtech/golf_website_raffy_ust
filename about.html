ABOUT SPGA

San Pasucal Golfers (SPG) was started in 2009 where in members were all residents of San Pascual. It officially became San Pascual Golfers Association (SPGA) when it extended its membership outside San Pascual in 2010.

The tournaments and other events were conducted in San Pascual Golf Course which was considered as their home course.

Location: The San Pascual Golf club is a distinguished golf course in Batangas. Its operation began in 2012. Its short 9-hole course features flat fairways that are perfect for beginning golfers or those who choose to walk the course. Aligning the fairways of the course are three unique trees of the Acacia, Pine, and Narra families. In addition, you will find that the course is surrounded by narrow ravines, water hazards, and deep ditches that make the course extra challenging to golfers.
